Booking Process

1. Book a Consultation Slot

Reserve a 30-minute timeslot through Moodle. Only available on Wednesdays.

2. Prepare Your Design

Have your project plan and CAD design ready before the consultation. The demonstrator will review your files.

3. Consultation Meeting

Present your plan and CAD during the session. Discuss feasibility and any modifications needed.

4. Get Approval

Gain approval from the demonstrator. They will verify your design meets safety and machine requirements.

5. Manufacturing

Once approved, proceed with CNC machining or vacuum forming. Your demonstrator will handle the CAM setup based on your CAD.

Equipment Information

CNC Machine Specifications

  • Work Area: 600mm x 400mm x 100mm
  • Materials: Wood, MDF, Acrylic, Foam, Soft Metals
  • Precision: ¬±0.1mm accuracy
  • File Formats: DXF, SVG, STL (for 3D)

Best For

  • Wing ribs and structural components
  • Mounting brackets and fixtures
  • Custom jigs and templates
  • Precise geometric cuts

Vacuum Former Specifications

  • Forming Area: 400mm x 300mm
  • Max Depth: 150mm draw depth
  • Materials: PETG, HIPS, ABS sheets
  • Sheet Thickness: 0.5mm - 3mm

Best For

  • Aerodynamic wing skins
  • Smooth curved surfaces
  • Lightweight covers and fairings
  • Repeatable plastic parts
